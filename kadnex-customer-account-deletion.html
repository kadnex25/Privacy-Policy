Account Deletion Request

If you want to delete your account and associated data from Kadnex, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Profile by clicking menu icon in the top left coloumn
  3. Tap "Delete Account" option at the bootom

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by contacting us via email:

📧 kadnex25@email.com

Data Deletion Details

  • All personal data associated with your account will be permanently deleted.

Processing Time

Your request will be processed within 7 days.
